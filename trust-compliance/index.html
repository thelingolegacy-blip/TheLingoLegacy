Gate

Role separation.

No single person should have unrestricted access across safety, economy, moderation, and support operations.

Access ladder

Owner → Admin → Moderator → Support

Owner handles final approvals and account-level controls. Admins operate dashboards. Moderators review content and disputes. Support handles guided user actions without broad economy or security power.

Audit rule

Every privileged action leaves a trail

Major economy edits, account actions, content decisions, scoring disputes, and launch-gate changes need actor, timestamp, reason, and rollback notes.
