How It Works

Each spin uses weighted symbols across a configurable 5x3 grid. Wins are evaluated across the three horizontal rows. WILD substitutes for regular symbols. Three or more SCATTER symbols trigger a demo bonus award. MULT can apply a visual multiplier to qualifying wins.
