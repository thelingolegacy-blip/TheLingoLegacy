Safety Checklist

Use before broad classroom, family, youth, rewards, or app-store rollout. This checklist supports the current static-only site and future privacy gates.

No child accounts, cloud profiles, or login prompts are active.
No forms collect child names, emails, locations, photos, voice, or identifiers.
No paid telemetry, AI calls, ads, tracking pixels, or third-party scripts are added.
Activities are adult-guided and suitable for the intended age group.
Rewards are cosmetic, local, and have no cash value or prize language.
Future storage, forms, analytics, or app builds wait for privacy and compliance review.

Future backend gate

Before enabling persistent email capture, child profiles, cloud saves, leaderboards, analytics, or AI-generated content, complete privacy review, youth-safety review, security review, and clear parent/guardian consent design.
