Lesson Plan

Use with Sunny Key, Blue Clue Trail, or a future static episode. Adult-led, no login, no data collection.

Grade band: Early learner
Time: 20–30 minutes
Mode: Read, play, reflect
Objective

Practice courage, focus, sequence order, helper language, and gentle problem solving.

Materials

Episode page, clue cards, paper/pencil, and an adult facilitator.

Safety note

Keep activities adult-guided. Do not ask children to submit personal information.

Success check

Learner names one clue, one helper, one feeling, and one next step.

Session timeline

0–5 minPick helper character and preview the story goal.
5–15 minRead episode pages aloud and pause at each clue.
15–25 minUse clue cards to sequence the story and discuss helper choices.
25–30 minClose with one brave sentence or focus sentence.
