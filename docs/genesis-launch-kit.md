# Lingo Legacy™ Genesis Launch Kit

This launch kit turns the Sprint roadmap into the first public asset: TheLingoLegacy.com HQ, first revenue storefront sections, Kotton’s Code™ digital product listings, Lingo.ai™ founders waitlist, community signup, and a 30-day rollout plan.

## Launch Day 0 Foundation

### Public HQ

Deploy these sections first:
- TheLingoLegacy.com homepage.
- Entity showcase pages or homepage sections.
- Coming-soon countdown and launch status.
- Email signup and community signup.
- Privacy policy, terms of service, contact, and support pages.

### First Revenue Launch

Open Loyalty Lane Apparel™ first because it creates the earliest revenue path.

Founder Collection:

- Shadow Noir™ Premium Collection.

Store setup needed before accepting orders:
- Storefront.
- Product pages.
- Checkout.

Revenue model:
- Pre-orders.
- Limited drops.
- Membership access.

### Kotton’s Code™ Digital Store

## Production Integration Checklist

Before collecting real customer data or taking payments, connect:
- Email capture provider or backend endpoint.
- Commerce checkout provider.
- Order confirmation emails.
- Digital delivery flow.
- Analytics tracking.
- Privacy policy and terms pages reviewed for production use.
- Support inbox or ticketing workflow.

The static forms in the launch page are frontend-ready placeholders. They intentionally do not send data until a production capture provider or backend endpoint is connected.
