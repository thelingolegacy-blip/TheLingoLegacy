# Lingo Safety Core v1.0

Lingo Safety Core is the shared Trust Layer for That’s My Lingo, Lingo Draft, Loyalty Lane rewards, Lingo ID, Universal Wallet, community platforms, and Kotton’s Code.

## Guardrail lanes

- User Protection: secure authentication, email/device verification, optional MFA, session monitoring, suspicious-login alerts, and protected account recovery.
- Age Controls: signup age gate, region-based rules, parental controls for kid-focused products, and separated child/adult experiences.
- Fair Play Engine: bot detection, abnormal behavior review, draft timing analysis, transparent scoring, leaderboard audits, dispute reporting, and season resets.
- Privacy & Security: encryption in transit, secure database rules, least-privilege permissions, admin role separation, audit logs, and backup strategy.
- Content Moderation: report queues, review status, policy reasons, action history, and escalation flow.
- Economy Controls: currency creation/spending logs, reward frequency, inflation monitoring, duplicate transaction prevention, refunds, daily limits, and approval gates.
- Responsible Play: session reminders, activity history, optional limits, break reminders, help resources, and easy account controls.
- Compliance Monitoring: release readiness, legal copy, app store review, analytics verification, abuse testing, load testing, and rollback ownership.

## No-exploitation product rules

Avoid manipulative countdown pressure, false scarcity, misleading rewards, hidden costs, and aggressive purchase prompts. Require clear pricing, clear odds/chances where applicable, purchase confirmation, and spending history visibility.

## Command Center roles

Owner → Admin → Moderator → Support. No single person should have unrestricted access across account actions, moderation, economy changes, and compliance gates. Major privileged actions need actor, timestamp, reason, and rollback notes.

## Launch gate

Before public launch, verify security review, privacy review, completed Terms, active user reporting, verified analytics, tested backups, abuse testing, load testing, and app store compliance review.

This is an operating blueprint, not legal advice. Fantasy, rewards, youth, paid subscription, virtual economy, or competitive entertainment surfaces need qualified legal and compliance review before public launch.
