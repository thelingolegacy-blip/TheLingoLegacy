# That’s My Lingo Social Casino Demo — Production Roadmap

This roadmap keeps the product in a non-gambling model: demo coins, XP, badges, cosmetics, and progression only. Virtual currency has no cash value and cannot be redeemed for money or prizes.

## Phase 1 — Gameplay foundation
- Configurable 5x3 reel model with weighted reel strips.
- Paytable with wild, scatter, multiplier, and five-of-a-kind outcomes.
- Demo coin economy with bet selector, session stats, and win history.
- Clear RTP/balance testing notes for demo tuning only.
- Help and paytable screens visible in the game UI.

## Phase 2 — Production polish
- High-resolution original symbols, animated reels, stronger background floor art, lighting, shadows, particles, and logo animation.
- Audio pass: music, reel start/stop, win tiers, jackpot celebration, bonus music, UI clicks, ambient effects, and volume controls.
- Accessibility controls for sound, animation intensity, contrast, and touch targets.

## Phase 3 — Progression and live loops
- Player level, XP, titles, badges, daily objectives, weekly objectives, achievement system, reward calendar, collection book, and milestone rewards.
- Daily missions, weekly events, holiday themes, limited-time cosmetics, community goals, and seasonal campaigns.

## Phase 4 — Backend and security
- Firebase Authentication, Firestore cloud save, player profiles, inventory, daily rewards, XP, achievements, leaderboards, analytics, crash reporting, Remote Config, and Cloud Functions.
- Firebase App Check, server-authoritative rewards, rate limiting, anti-cheat validation, encrypted save data, monitoring, backups, and audit logging.

## Phase 5 — Monetization without wagering
- Optional cosmetics, avatars, card frames, animation packs, starter bundles, VIP membership, battle pass, rewarded ads, and clearly labeled coin bundles where permitted.
- No cash-out, no wagering, no cash-value prizes, and no language implying real-money gambling.
- Use platform in-app purchase systems for app-store digital goods when required.

## Phase 6 — Release readiness
- Privacy Policy, Terms of Service, age rating, screenshots, feature graphics, promo video, store description, icons, keywords, closed beta, open testing, production monitoring, rollback strategy, and release notes.
