The brand spine preserves entity identity while keeping the Lingo Legacy OS connected.

- Lingo.ai — That’s My Lingo.

## Rules

- Use Industrial Noir as the global aesthetic.
- Preserve per-entity tone, colors, profile image, and banner rules.
- Link every entity back to the OS hub.
- Keep social bios and pinned posts aligned with the entity pitch.
