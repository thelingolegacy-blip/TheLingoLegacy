# Social launch calendar

This is the 30-day execution layer for the “That’s My Lingo” campaign.

## Week 1 — Awareness

- Teaser Reels/TikToks for quick gameplay and bold captions.
- Lingo Legacy launch thread and OS hub CTA.
- Avalon culture, language, and play intro.
- Peaches Keys character spotlight.

## Week 2 — Play & Share

- “Play That’s My Lingo” score challenge.
- Screenshot your score and tag the account.
- XP, shoutout, and badge language staged for follow-up.

## Week 3 — Deep Dive

- YouTube and LinkedIn OS explainer.
- Avalon nonprofit tie-in.
- XP/rewards explainer for Lingo.ai.

## Week 4 — Consolidation

- Community compilation.
- Best plays montage.
- Rollout recap.
- Final CTA to bookmark the hub and follow all entities.
