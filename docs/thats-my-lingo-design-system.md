# That’s My Lingo — Design System and Asset Catalog

That’s My Lingo uses a premium social-casino identity: deep black backgrounds, metallic gold frames, neon purple highlights, gold typography, Bridgeton-to-Philadelphia progression, and community-themed locations. The product remains entertainment-only: virtual currency has no cash value and cannot be redeemed for money or prizes.

## Visual language
- Palette: black base, metallic gold UI, neon purple accents, cyan glow states, green reward confirmations.
- Surfaces: full-screen floors, glowing reel frames, roadmap worlds, premium lobby cards, and cinematic event banners.
- Motion: reel spin, reel stop, win flash, coin burst, neon pulse, button press, reward chest opening, trophy pop-up, and reduced-motion alternatives.
- Typography: bold uppercase display for headings, compact labels for wallet balances, readable body copy for compliance and help.

## UI asset library
- Main lobby, login, profile, wallet, reward shop, daily bonus, settings, friends, leaderboard, achievements, collections, events, mailbox, notifications.
- Buttons, badges, tabs, wallet pills, mission tiles, pass tracks, notification toasts, modal frames, and accessible focus states.

## Reel asset catalog
- Reel frame: 5x3 premium metallic frame with win glow and reduced-motion fallback.
- Common symbols: L, I, N, G, O, Bulldog, Graduation Cap, Cheesesteak, Vinyl Record, Trophy, Bridge, Street Sign, Gold Coin.
- Premium symbols: Gold Bulldog, Purple Star, Legacy Trophy, Neon Skyline, Jackpot Vault, Lingo Crown, Diamond L.
- Special symbols: Wild, Expanding Wild, Scatter, Bonus, Multipliers 2x/3x/5x/10x/25x/50x/100x, Collect, Mystery Symbol.

## World progression
1. Bridgeton: Bulldogs, downtown, river bridge, high school, local businesses, graduation.
2. Philadelphia: LOVE Park, Broad Street, South Philly, City Hall, Museum District, Championship Event.
3. Future: Atlantic City, New York, Miami, Las Vegas, Jamaica, London, Tokyo, Dubai.

Each world should unlock new reel skins, soundtrack, symbols, achievements, seasonal events, collectibles, and 25-50 level milestones.

## Reward shop and passes

- Membership tiers: Bronze, Silver, Gold, Founder. Benefits stay cosmetic/progression-only.
- Daily rewards: Coins, XP, Cosmetic Chest, Bonus Spins, Multiplier Token, Legendary Reward Chest.
