Lingo Legacy Treasury & Lingo.ai Connect

A product blueprint for subscription-first cash planning and secure AI orchestration across the Lingo Legacy ecosystem.

Lingo Legacy Treasury

A centralized financial management layer that receives business revenue, reserves required amounts, tracks subscriptions, and reports owner distributions after required obligations are covered.

  1. Tax reserve
  2. Required operating expenses
  3. Software subscriptions
  4. Payroll and contractors
  5. Marketing budget
  6. Emergency reserve
  7. Business savings
  8. Owner distribution

Subscription Manager

  • Vendor, category, amount, renewal date, and payment method label
  • Required versus optional status and priority level
  • Reminder schedule and cash coverage warnings
  • Monthly summaries and bookkeeping exports

Lingo.ai Connect

The integration layer for Ask Lingo: agents, permissions, APIs, notifications, and third-party app connections.

  • Business Agent for reports, inventory, messages, and scheduling
  • Creator Agent for posts, scripts, videos, and marketing ideas
  • Developer Agent for code review, docs, testing, and deployment help
  • Personal Agent for reminders, notes, organization, and planning

Security Requirements

  • Strong authentication and explicit per-integration authorization
  • Encryption, audit logs, revocable tokens, and privacy controls
  • Role-based access and manual approval for high-risk actions
  • Accountant or advisor review for tax reserves and owner distributions

The system can prioritize payments and recommend distributions, but it cannot guarantee subscriptions are paid unless account balances, providers, and authorization rules support the payment.

Vercel-Native Implementation

  • Vercel Functions for APIs, reports, alerts, and automation handlers
  • Vercel Cron Jobs for renewal reminders and monthly summaries
  • Vercel Blob for reports and uploaded documents
  • Neon via the Vercel Marketplace for relational data. Vercel Postgres is no longer first-party; existing databases were migrated in December 2024.
  • Upstash Redis via the Vercel Marketplace for queues and short-lived workflow state. Vercel KV is no longer first-party; existing stores were migrated in December 2024.
  • Vercel AI Gateway for AI model routing and agent orchestration

First Milestone

  1. Add subscription records manually.
  2. Add monthly revenue and expense entries manually.
  3. Calculate reserves and projected owner distribution.
  4. Show upcoming renewals and cash coverage warnings.
  5. Export a monthly summary for bookkeeping review.
