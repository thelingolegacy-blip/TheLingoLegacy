12-Month Scale + Profit Model

Launch to traction to scale

Revenue targets are operating goals, not guarantees. Actual results depend on execution, budget, audience growth, pricing, fulfillment, and market response.

Month 1

Genesis Launch

TheLingoLegacy.com HQ, Loyalty Lane Apparel™ preorders, Kotton’s Code™ digital products, and Lingo.ai™ waitlist.

  • Gross target: $5K–$10K
  • Expenses: $2K–$5K
  • Net target: $2K–$5K
Months 2–3

First Revenue Engine

Activate Lingo.ai™ tiers, apparel drops, books, digital bundles, and audio previews.

  • Gross target: $15K–$50K/month
  • Net target: $5K–$20K/month
  • Drops: Tap Stitch™, Shadow Noir™, Legacy Builder™
Months 4–6

Ecosystem Growth

Launch That’s My Lingo™ revenue channels and expand Kotton’s Code™ interactive products.

  • Gross target: $50K–$150K/month
  • Net target: $15K–$60K/month
  • Ads, purchases, events, learning activities
Months 7–12

Scale Mode

Pilot Loyalty Lane Cycles™ and expand apparel, AI, games, partnerships, and loyalty programs.

  • Gross target: $100K–$500K/month
  • Net target: $30K–$200K/month
  • Memberships, partner fees, loyalty programs
