Sprints 1–5: Foundation

The core platform connects users, profiles, rewards, AI tools, and the first mobile shell.

Sprint 1 — Lingo ID™ Core Platform

Universal identity system for every product.

  • Email, Google, Apple, phone, recovery
  • Profiles, preferences, XP, rewards, membership
  • Permissions, sessions, account protection
  • Gate: signup, login, profile save, security rules

Sprint 2 — Universal Wallet™ + XP Engine™

Economy layer for rewards and progression.

  • Loyalty Bucks™ and Lingo Tokens™
  • XP from games, books, purchases, referrals
  • Wallets, transactions, levels, badges
  • Gate: balances, history, transactions, XP updates

Sprint 3 — Website MVP

Public headquarters for the ecosystem.

  • Homepage, products, community, support, login
  • Newsletter, announcements, SEO, analytics
  • Cards for Lingo.ai™, Ask Lingo™, games, books, apparel
  • Gate: deployed, responsive, analytics, signup

Sprint 4 — Lingo.ai™ MVP

AI assistant and creator tool foundation.

  • Ask Lingo chat, writing help, planning
  • Grammar, rewrite, summaries, tone changes
  • Stories, characters, marketing ideas
  • Gate: chat, history, usage tracking, subscription tiers

Sprint 5 — Ask Lingo™ Mobile App

Mobile experience for Lingo AI.

  • Launch, home, voice, tools, history, wallet, settings
  • Auth, AI chat, push, sync, dark mode
  • Flutter screens, widgets, services, models
  • Gate: shell, login, AI, synced user data

Sprints 6–10: Product Launches

The ecosystem expands into game beta, interactive learning, publishing, apparel commerce, and physical community pilots.

Sprint 6 — That’s My Lingo™ Slot Game Beta

First playable entertainment product.

  • HTML5, Phaser 3, Firebase
  • 5×3 reels, 20 paylines, auto spin, turbo, bonuses
  • Player → Lingo ID™ → profile → wallet → XP
  • Gate: playable beta, login, cloud save, rewards

Sprint 7 — Kotton’s Code™ Digital Universe

Interactive learning world.

  • Loyalty Lane™ Heart World, Hidden Code Realm™, Rift System™
  • Explore, learn words, complete quests, earn Code XP
  • Dialogue, quests, inventory, progression, rewards
  • Gate: first world, characters, learning activities

Sprint 8 — Publishing & Media Pipeline

Content production and digital delivery system.

  • Books, ebooks, audiobooks, activity books, digital stories
  • Say It Again™ speech practice products
  • Publishing HQ, assets, audio, metadata, distribution
  • Gate: products organized, downloads, store connection

Sprint 9 — Loyalty Lane Apparel™ Commerce

Fashion division launch.

  • Catalog, cart, checkout, inventory, orders
  • Tap Stitch™, Shadow Noir™, Legacy Builder™, Kotton’s Code™
  • Customer → Lingo ID™ → purchase → wallet rewards → XP
  • Gate: store live, payments tested, rewards connected

Sprint 10 — Loyalty Lane Cycles™ Pilot

Physical community operations.

  • Laundry and grocery partner services
  • Customer app for hubs, scheduling, tracking, rewards
  • Partner dashboard for orders, customers, promos, analytics
  • Gate: partners identified, pilot tested, signup ready

Sprints 11–15: Operations + Expansion

The roadmap closes with centralized marketing, administration, community impact, device expansion, and global operating systems.

Sprint 11 — Marketing War Room™

Promotion engine for every entity.

  • Marketing HQ™, campaigns, calendar, ads, email, SMS, push
  • Entity campaigns for Lingo.ai™, Ask Lingo™, That’s My Lingo™, Kotton’s Code™, Loyalty Lane™
  • Morning awareness, afternoon engagement, evening retention
  • Gate: campaign DB, calendar, notifications, analytics

Sprint 12 — Admin HQ™ Command Center

Operating dashboard for the ecosystem.

  • Users, commerce, games, content, partners
  • Track users, revenue, subscriptions, downloads, game activity, sales, partner growth
  • Management tools and reporting
  • Gate: admin access, reporting, tools, operations dashboard

Sprint 13 — Iconic House of Avalon™

Community impact division.

  • Youth support, donations, memberships
  • Volunteer, partner, sponsor signup
  • Events, registration, updates, volunteer tracking
  • Gate: structure, membership flow, programs, reports

Sprint 14 — Platform Expansion

More devices and distribution channels.

  • Smart TVs, streaming platforms, web apps, tablets
  • Mobile, web, and TV gaming experiences
  • Animated shorts, YouTube content, digital experiences
  • Gate: architecture, device strategy, media plan

Sprint 15 — Global Launch Operations

Scale-ready operating model.

  • Support, product updates, marketing, analytics, partnerships
  • Referral engine, XP, rewards, exclusive access
  • Ambassadors, documentation, training, vendor management
  • Gate: global launch and regional partner model

Final State

A single Lingo ID™ connects AI, games, books, apparel, Loyalty Lane Cycles™, community programs, and global partner operations.
